# $100M Leads — Alex Hormozi

## Core Principle
Leads are the lifeblood of every business. There are only four ways to get them — two you do yourself (outbound), two others do for you (inbound). Master all four, and you'll never worry about where your next client comes from.

## The Core Four Lead Generation Methods

### 1. Warm Outreach (You → People Who Know You)
Reaching out to people who already know you exist.

**Channels:** DMs, texts, calls, emails to your existing network
**The "ACA" Framework:**
- **Acknowledge** — Reference something personal/specific
- **Compliment** — Genuine, not salesy
- **Ask** — "Hey, I'm working on [thing]. Know anyone who [ideal client description]?"

**Key rules:**
- Never pitch on first contact — build the conversation
- Ask for referrals, not sales
- Volume matters: 100 reach-outs/day minimum when starting
- Your personal network is your first goldmine — use it

### 2. Cold Outreach (You → Strangers)
Reaching out to people who don't know you yet.

**Channels:** Cold email, cold DM, cold call, LinkedIn outreach
**The framework:**
1. **Build a targeted list** — Specific industry, role, company size
2. **Personalize the first line** — Show you did research
3. **Lead with value** — Offer something helpful before asking for anything
4. **Clear CTA** — One specific next step ("Worth a 15-min call?")
5. **Follow up relentlessly** — Most deals close between follow-up 5–12

**Key rules:**
- Volume: 100+ cold outreach per day
- Personalization beats automation
- Test subject lines, opening lines, and CTAs
- Track reply rates and optimize weekly

### 3. Content (Others → You, Free)
Creating content that attracts your ideal clients to you.

**The "Give Away the Secrets" Strategy:**
- Share your best stuff for free — it builds trust and authority
- "Free content sells paid implementation"
- Post daily on platforms where your ideal clients hang out

**Content types that generate leads:**
- **How-to** — Teach one specific thing
- **Proof** — Show results, case studies, before/after
- **Contrarian** — Challenge a common belief in your industry
- **Story** — Your journey, your clients' journeys
- **List** — "7 ways to...", "5 mistakes that..."

**Volume:** 1+ piece of content per day, minimum

### 4. Paid Ads (Others → You, Paid)
Using advertising to reach your ideal clients at scale.

**The framework:**
1. **Start with a lead magnet** — Free offer that attracts ideal clients
2. **Run ads to the lead magnet** — Optimize for cost per lead
3. **Nurture with content** — Email sequence, retargeting
4. **Convert with a core offer** — Sales page, call booking, webinar

**Key rules:**
- Start with one platform, master it, then expand
- Test creatives aggressively (new ads weekly)
- Track cost per lead AND cost per acquisition
- Profitable ads = scale. Unprofitable ads = fix the funnel, not the ad.

## The Lead Magnet Framework

A great lead magnet:
1. **Solves a specific problem** for your ideal client
2. **Delivers a quick win** — value in under 10 minutes
3. **Reveals a bigger gap** — shows them they need more help
4. **Qualifies the lead** — attracts the right people, repels the wrong ones

**Types:**
- Free training/webinar
- PDF guide/checklist
- Free tool/calculator
- Free audit/assessment
- Free sample/trial

## The Scaling Formula

**More leads = More money** but only if:
- Your offer converts (Grand Slam Offer)
- Your fulfillment delivers (happy clients = referrals)
- Your LTV > CAC (lifetime value exceeds acquisition cost)

Scale by:
1. Max out one channel before adding another
2. Increase volume before optimizing conversion
3. Layer channels: warm outreach + content + cold outreach + ads

## Key Insight
"The business that can spend the most to acquire a customer wins. But you can only spend more if your offer is so good that clients stay, pay more, and refer others. Leads without a great offer is a leaky bucket. Fix the offer first, then flood the leads."
