# The 6 Human Needs — Tony Robbins

## Core Principle
Every human decision — including every buying decision — is driven by the need to fulfill one or more of these six needs. The first four are "needs of the personality." The last two are "needs of the spirit."

## The 6 Needs

### 1. Certainty (Comfort & Security)
The need to feel safe, stable, and sure of what's coming.

- In business: Guarantees, proven systems, testimonials, risk reversal
- Limiting belief: "I need to know it will work before I invest"
- Coaching move: Show the system, the proof, the path

### 2. Variety (Uncertainty & Excitement)
The need for change, challenge, surprise, and novelty.

- In business: New strategies, fresh approaches, excitement about possibility
- Limiting belief: "I've tried everything — nothing new will work"
- Coaching move: Reframe their situation — show them what they haven't tried

### 3. Significance (Feeling Important & Unique)
The need to feel special, needed, important, worthy.

- In business: Personal branding, being seen as an expert, competitive edge
- Limiting belief: "Who am I to charge that much / play that big?"
- Coaching move: Anchor their unique value — no one else has their exact combination

### 4. Love & Connection (Belonging)
The need to feel connected, loved, and part of something.

- In business: Community, team, client relationships, purpose-driven work
- Limiting belief: "Success means isolation / I'll lose my relationships"
- Coaching move: Show how growth attracts better relationships

### 5. Growth (Expansion & Progress)
The need to develop, learn, and become more.

- In business: Skill development, revenue milestones, personal evolution
- Key driver: If someone isn't growing, they feel like they're dying
- Coaching move: Frame the offer as a growth vehicle, not just a purchase

### 6. Contribution (Giving Beyond Yourself)
The need to serve, give back, and make a difference.

- In business: Impact, legacy, mission-driven branding, client transformation
- Key driver: The deepest fulfillment comes from contribution
- Coaching move: Connect their offer to the lives they'll change

## Application in Sales & Coaching

1. **Identify the prospect's top 2 needs** — listen for language patterns
2. **Frame your offer** through the lens of those needs
3. **Address the limiting belief** that blocks each need from being met
4. **Use the needs hierarchy** to create emotional buying momentum

## Key Insight
"The quality of your life is in direct proportion to the quality of the questions you ask yourself. Change the questions, change the focus, change the life."
