# Peak State Performance — Tony Robbins

## Core Principle
Peak performance is not accidental. It's a state you can trigger on demand. The highest achievers don't wait to "feel motivated" — they create the state they need through deliberate physiology, focus, and rituals.

## The Hour of Power (Morning Priming)

A daily ritual to set your state before the world sets it for you:

### 10 Minutes: Move & Breathe
- Intense breathing exercise (e.g., 3 sets of 30 power breaths)
- Movement: jumping, walking, push-ups — anything to shift physiology
- Purpose: flood the body with oxygen, shift from sleep state to peak state

### 10 Minutes: Gratitude & Visualization
- 3 moments you're grateful for (feel them fully — don't just list them)
- Visualize your goals as already achieved — see it, feel it, hear it
- "Step into" your future self — how do they move, speak, decide?

### 10 Minutes: Incantations & Focus
- Repeat empowering beliefs with full emotional intensity (not affirmations — incantations are felt in the body)
- Set your top 3 intentions for the day
- Ask: "What must I accomplish today to make this day a win?"

## Emotional Mastery for Entrepreneurs

### The Emotions That Build Businesses
- **Certainty** — "I know this will work" → enables bold action
- **Hunger** — "I must have this" → creates relentless drive
- **Gratitude** — "I'm grateful for what I have" → prevents desperation in sales
- **Playfulness** — "This is a game" → reduces pressure, increases creativity
- **Love** — "I genuinely care about my clients" → creates authentic connection

### The Emotions That Destroy Businesses
- **Fear** → leads to inaction, underpricing, hiding
- **Overwhelm** → leads to paralysis, busy-work, avoidance of the important
- **Unworthiness** → leads to discounting, over-delivering, inability to sell
- **Frustration** → leads to quitting too early, blaming external factors
- **Complacency** → leads to coasting, losing edge, declining standards

### The Pattern Interrupt
When caught in a destructive emotional pattern:
1. **Notice it** — "I'm in fear/overwhelm/unworthiness right now"
2. **Break the pattern** — Change physiology immediately (stand up, move, cold water, loud music)
3. **Replace it** — Ask an empowering question: "What's great about this situation?"
4. **Anchor** — Associate the new state with a physical trigger (fist pump, deep breath, power pose)

## Decision-Making at Peak State

Never make important business decisions from a low state:
- Don't set prices when you feel unworthy
- Don't respond to difficult clients when you're frustrated
- Don't evaluate your business when you're exhausted
- Don't make strategic pivots when you're afraid

Instead: Change your state FIRST, then decide.

## Energy Management for Entrepreneurs

Energy is your #1 asset. Protect it like revenue.

**Energy creators:**
- Morning ritual (non-negotiable)
- Exercise (daily, even 20 minutes)
- Nutrition that fuels performance
- Sleep (7–9 hours — not optional)
- Time in nature
- Connection with high-energy people

**Energy destroyers:**
- Decision fatigue (batch decisions, create systems)
- Toxic relationships (set boundaries or exit)
- News/social media consumption (limit or eliminate)
- Saying yes to everything (protect your calendar)
- Perfectionism (progress over perfection)

## Key Insight
"The difference between where you are and where you want to be is not information — it's state. You already know what to do. You need to be in a state where you actually do it. Master your state, master your life."
