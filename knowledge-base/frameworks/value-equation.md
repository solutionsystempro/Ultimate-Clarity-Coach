# The Value Equation — Alex Hormozi

## Core Formula

**Value = (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood of Achievement) ÷ (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice)**

## The Four Variables

### 1. Dream Outcome (Maximize)
The ideal end result the customer wants. Not just "lose weight" but "look amazing at the beach in 12 weeks with confidence."

- What is the absolute best-case scenario for your customer?
- How can you paint that picture vividly?
- What would they pay anything for?

### 2. Perceived Likelihood of Achievement (Maximize)
How confident the prospect is that they will actually get the result.

Increase through:
- **Social proof** — testimonials, case studies, before/after
- **Credibility** — your track record, credentials, specificity
- **Risk reversal** — guarantees, pay-on-results models
- **Mechanism** — a clear, believable system that explains WHY it works

### 3. Time Delay (Minimize)
How long between purchase and result. Shorter = more valuable.

Reduce through:
- Quick wins in the first 24–48 hours
- Clear milestones showing progress
- Faster delivery mechanisms
- "Speed bonuses" for early action

### 4. Effort & Sacrifice (Minimize)
What the customer has to give up or do. Less friction = more valuable.

Reduce through:
- Done-for-you vs. done-with-you vs. do-it-yourself (DFY is highest value)
- Templates, scripts, swipe files
- Removing steps from the process
- Making it enjoyable, not painful

## Application

When building an offer, score each variable 1–10 and identify which one is the weakest. That's your highest-leverage improvement.

## Key Insight
"People don't buy because of price. They don't buy because of perceived value. Make the value so obvious that price becomes irrelevant."
