# The Strategy of Preeminence — Jay Abraham

## Core Principle
The Strategy of Preeminence is the highest expression of business growth: position yourself as the most trusted, most valued advisor in your market — not by claiming it, but by BEING it. Fall in love with your client, not your product.

## The 4 Pillars of Preeminence

### 1. See Yourself as Your Client's Trusted Advisor
Not a vendor. Not a salesperson. A fiduciary — someone who puts the client's interests first, always.

**In practice:**
- Recommend what's best for THEM, even if it's not your most profitable option
- Tell them when they DON'T need your service
- Over-deliver consistently
- Educate before you sell
- Your goal is their success, not your commission

### 2. Fall in Love with Your Client, Not Your Product
Most businesses are product-centric. Preeminent businesses are client-centric.

**The shift:**
- From "How do I sell more?" → "How do I serve better?"
- From "What's my revenue target?" → "What's the best outcome for my client?"
- From "How do I get more leads?" → "How do I create more value for the people I already serve?"

### 3. Always Give First
The preeminent business gives before it receives. This is not charity — it's strategy.

**Why it works:**
- Reciprocity: people want to return value to those who give first
- Trust: generosity signals confidence, not desperation
- Demonstration: showing your value is 10x more persuasive than claiming it

**How to give first:**
- Free valuable content (education marketing)
- Free consultations/audits
- Referrals to other providers (even competitors) when it serves the client
- Risk reversal (guarantees) — you take the risk, not them

### 4. Lead with Empathy and Understanding
Understand your client's world better than they understand it themselves.

**The deep understanding:**
- Their fears (what keeps them up at night)
- Their desires (what they dream about)
- Their frustrations (what's not working)
- Their aspirations (who they want to become)
- Their constraints (time, money, knowledge, confidence)

**When you articulate their problem better than they can, they automatically trust you with the solution.**

## The Three Ways to Grow (Applied with Preeminence)

### 1. More Clients (but the RIGHT clients)
- Referral systems powered by genuine delight (not incentive programs)
- Joint ventures based on shared values, not just shared audiences
- Thought leadership that attracts your ideal client naturally
- Risk reversal that removes every barrier to the first purchase

### 2. Higher Transaction Value (through genuine added value)
- Bundle complementary services that solve the WHOLE problem
- Premium tiers for clients who want deeper access
- Done-for-you options that remove effort from the client
- Strategic upsells that genuinely improve the outcome

### 3. More Frequent Purchases (through ongoing relationship)
- Continuity/membership that delivers perpetual value
- Regular check-ins and proactive outreach
- New offers developed based on client feedback
- Community that keeps clients engaged between purchases

## The Hidden Assets Framework

Every business has underleveraged assets:

**Asset Audit:**
1. **Client list** — Who hasn't heard from you recently? Who bought once but not again?
2. **Relationships** — Who do you know that serves your ideal client? (joint venture opportunity)
3. **Expertise** — What do you know that could be a new product/service?
4. **Content** — What could be repurposed, repackaged, or redistributed?
5. **Processes** — What systems have you built that others would pay for?
6. **Testimonials** — Where could social proof be amplified?
7. **Dead leads** — Who said "not now" that could become "yes" with a different offer?

## The Joint Venture Framework

The fastest path to growth: borrow someone else's audience and trust.

**The Host-Beneficiary Model:**
1. Identify a "host" — someone who already serves your ideal client
2. Create a win-win-win offer (host wins, you win, client wins)
3. Host endorses your offer to their audience
4. You fulfill, they earn a referral fee or reciprocal endorsement
5. Client gets a vetted recommendation from someone they trust

**Why it works:** You're not building trust from scratch — you're borrowing it.

## Key Insight
"The Strategy of Preeminence is not a tactic — it's a philosophy. When you genuinely care more about your client's success than your own revenue, paradoxically, your revenue grows faster than ever. The market always rewards the advisor who puts the client first. Always."
